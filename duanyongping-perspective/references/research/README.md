# Duan Yongping (段永平) Research Directory

This directory contains the distilled research on Duan Yongping's thinking style, Benfen business culture, "dare to be last" product strategy, value investment mindset (inspired by Warren Buffett), and Stop-Doing List.
The research is organized into 6 dimensions:
- `01-writings.md`: Speeches, compiled Q&As (Xueqiu blog @东莞阿段), owner's principles, system-level thinking.
- `02-conversations.md`: Interviews (e.g. with financial media, PKU lectures), informal discussions, and Xueqiu interactive posts.
- `03-expression-dna.md`: Plain, simple, common-sense communication DNA, pet phrases ("本分", "敢为天下后", "买股票就是买公司"), tone.
- `04-external-views.md`: Commentaries by BBK/OPPO/VIVO disciples (Chen Mingyong, Shen Wei, Jin Zhijiang, Huang Zheng), peer reviews, and critical reviews of his investing style.
- `05-decisions.md`: Major corporate and investment decisions (founding BBK, split into OPPO/VIVO/BBK Education, early retirement to US, investing in NetEase in 2002, Apple, Tencent, Moutai).
- `06-timeline.md`: Chronological life timeline, milestone tracker.
