# Li Lu (李录) Research Directory

This directory contains the distilled research on Li Lu's thinking style, value investment mindset, civilization and modernization theories, and decision heuristics.
The research is organized into 6 dimensions:
- `01-writings.md`: Books (e.g. 《文明、现代化、价值投资与中国》), shareholder letters, essays, system-level thinking.
- `02-conversations.md`: Speeches at Columbia University, Peking University, podcasts, long Q&As.
- `03-expression-dna.md`: Phrasing, tone, structural communication DNA, vocabulary.
- `04-external-views.md`: Biography, reviews, comments by Charlie Munger, Warren Buffett, and value investing peer reviews.
- `05-decisions.md`: Major investment and career decisions (founding Himalaya Capital, investing in BYD, rejecting managing Berkshire fund, etc.).
- `06-timeline.md`: Chronological life timeline, milestone tracker.
